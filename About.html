ABOUT ME

Alex O. Akinjiola

UI/UX & Product Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria

With over 4 years of experience in product design, I specialize in creating user-centered digital experiences that solve real problems and drive business growth.

My approach combines deep user research with strategic thinking to deliver products that are not just beautiful, but functional and scalable. I've had the privilege of working across diverse industries including SaaS, Fintech, EdTech, Real Estate, and E-commerce.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring the latest design trends, mentoring aspiring designers, or enjoying a good book on product strategy.

I'm passionate about using design to make a positive impact and am always excited to connect with fellow designers, product teams, and potential collaborators. Let's create something amazing together. Currently, I'm a UI/UX Designer at Morgen Green, where I lead design initiatives for education technology and real estate platforms serving organizations across Nigeria.
